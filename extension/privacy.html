Ellyn Privacy & AI Disclosure

Last updated: February 2026

What we collect

Ellyn reads publicly visible information on LinkedIn profiles you visit - including name, job title, company, and profile URL - only when you actively click "Find Email". This data is saved to your personal Ellyn account and is never sold or shared with third parties.

AI usage

Email drafts are generated using Claude (Anthropic, including Haiku models). Profile information you have saved may be sent to the AI model to personalize outreach. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. No data is used to train AI models.

Email finding

Ellyn infers likely email addresses based on name patterns and company domains. Inferred emails are not guaranteed to be accurate. Email verification uses a third-party validation service (Abstract API) to check deliverability.

Data storage

Your contacts are stored in your private Ellyn account (Supabase). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. You can delete your contacts and account at any time from the dashboard.

LinkedIn compliance

Ellyn reads only what is publicly visible on LinkedIn pages you navigate to. It does not scrape, crawl, or automate LinkedIn in any way that violates their Terms of Service. You remain in full control of what is captured.
