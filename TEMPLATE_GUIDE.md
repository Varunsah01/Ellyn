# Template Guide

This guide explains how to create high-performing outreach templates in Ellyn.

## Recommended Workflow

1. Choose a preset template from the library.
2. Adjust subject + body for your target role/company.
3. Use variables for personalization:
- `{{firstName}}`
- `{{company}}`
- `{{role}}`
- `{{userName}}`
- `{{userSchool}}`
4. Use AI actions to refine tone/length/clarity.
5. Review live preview.
6. Save as a custom template.

## Preset Categories

- To Recruiter
- Referral Request
- Seeking Advice
- AI Generated
- Follow-Up
- Alumni Connection
- Thank You
- Startup Outreach

## Writing Best Practices

1. Keep emails short (80-150 words for first outreach).
2. Use one clear ask (call, referral, quick advice).
3. Mention a specific reason for contacting this person.
4. Avoid generic mass-outreach language.
5. End with a polite next step.

## Subject Line Tips

- Keep under 60 characters when possible.
- Mention role/company for relevance.
- Avoid spammy words (all caps, excessive punctuation).

## AI Tips

- Use "Enhance" after drafting your own first version.
- Use "Change Tone" to adapt for recruiter vs employee.
- Use "Start from Scratch" when context is clear but draft is empty.

## Important

- Never auto-send without review.
- Validate variable placeholders before use.
- Keep template claims truthful and specific.
